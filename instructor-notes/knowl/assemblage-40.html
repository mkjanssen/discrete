Binomial Coefficients.

For each integer \(n \ge 0\) and integer \(k\) with \(0 \le k \le n\) there is a number

\begin{equation*} {n\choose k}\text{,} \end{equation*}

read “\(n\) choose \(k\text{.}\)” We define \({n \choose k}\) to be the number of ways to select \(k\) objects from a total of \(n\) objects.
