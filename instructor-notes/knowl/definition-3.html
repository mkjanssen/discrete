Definition 9.3.1.

Consider a sequence \((a_n)_{n=0}^\infty\text{.}\) The first difference of \((a_n)\) is the sequence \(\Delta^1(a_n) = a_1 - a_0, a_2 - a_1, \ldots\text{.}\) The \(k\)-th difference sequence is the sequence

\begin{equation*} \Delta^k(a_n) = \Delta^{k-1}(a_n)_1 - \Delta^{k-1}(a_n)_0, \Delta^{k-1}(a_n)_2 - \Delta^{k-1}(a_n)_1, \ldots. \end{equation*}
