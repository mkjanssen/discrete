Item 2.
The sequence is \(1, 4, 10, 20, 35, \ldots\text{.}\) The first difference is \(3, 6, 10, 15\ldots\text{,}\) so the second is \(3, 4, 5\text{,}\) an arithmetic sequence. Thus, the sequence is \(\Delta^3\)-constant and will be described by a cubic, \(P_n = a n^3 + b n^2 + cn + d\text{.}\)
