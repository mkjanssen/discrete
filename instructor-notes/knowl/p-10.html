  1. The integer 3 is odd.

  4. \(x^2+4x=9\text{.}\)
  5. \(\displaystyle 0=1\)
  6. All cats are grey.
  7. All cats are adorable.
  8. This is really difficult!
  9. (Challenge) This statement is false.
