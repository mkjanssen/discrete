There are three main styles of proof we'll discuss (aside from combinatorial and inductive proofs, which we've already looked at): direct proofs, proofs by contradiction, and proofs by contrapositive. Direct proofs are the most common, while proofs by contradiction are perhaps the most fun, and proofs by contrapositive feel a bit like magic. There are other special techniques as well, and we'll discuss them as we encounter examples of them.
