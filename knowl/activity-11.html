Activity 6.1.1.

Recall that a binomial is an algebraic expression with two terms, such as \(x+y\text{.}\) Calculate the following.

  1. \(\displaystyle (x+y)^1\)
  2. \(\displaystyle (x+y)^2\)
  3. \(\displaystyle (x+y)^3\)
  4. \(\displaystyle (x+y)^4\)
  5. \(\displaystyle (x+y)^5\)

What do you notice? What do you wonder?
