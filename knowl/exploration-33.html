Exploration 7.2.2.

Find combinatorial proofs for the following identities. Bonus points for humor and creativity.

  1. \begin{equation*} n\binom{n-1}{k} = (k+1) \binom{n}{k+1} \end{equation*}
  2. \begin{equation*} n\binom{n-1}{k-1} = k\binom{n}{k} \end{equation*}
  3. \begin{equation*} n(n-1)\binom{n-2}{k-2} = k(k-1)\binom{n}{k} \end{equation*}
