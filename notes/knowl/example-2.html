Example 4.1.2.

Consider the following relations. Are they functions? Why or why not?

  1. \(X=\set{0,1,5}, Y = \set{\clubsuit, \spadesuit, \diamondsuit, \heartsuit}\text{,}\) \(f_1 = \set{(1,\clubsuit), (0, \heartsuit), (5,\heartsuit)}\)
  2. \(X=\set{0,1,5}, Y = \set{\clubsuit, \spadesuit, \diamondsuit, \heartsuit}\text{,}\) \(f_2 = \set{(1,\clubsuit), (0, \heartsuit), (0,\diamondsuit)}\)
  3. \(X = \Z\) (the integers), \(Y = \N\text{,}\) \(f_3 = \setof{(x,x^2)}{x\in X}\)
