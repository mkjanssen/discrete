Example A.1.2.

Lila's Sweet Shop sells \(m\) types of ice cream and \(n\) types of frozen yogurt. There are then \(m+n\) ways to get a single scoop of frozen yogurt or ice cream (additive principle) and \(m\cdot n\) ways to get one scoop of frozen yogurt and one scoop of ice cream (multiplicative principle).
