Exploration 11.1.2.

Are the following pairs of statements logically equivalent? Justify.

  1. \(P\land (Q\lor R)\) and \((P\land Q) \lor (P\land R)\)
  2. \(P\lor (Q\land R)\) and \((P\lor Q) \land (P\lor R)\)
  3. \(\neg(P\land Q)\) and \((\neg P) \lor (\neg Q)\)
  4. \(\neg(P\lor Q)\) and \((\neg P) \land (\neg Q)\)
  5. \(P\imp Q\) and \((\neg P) \lor Q\)
  6. \(\neg (P\imp Q)\) and \(P\land (\neg Q)\)
  7. \(P\imp (Q\lor R)\) and \((P\land (\neg Q))\imp R\)
  8. \(P\imp Q\) and \(Q\imp P\)
  9. \(P\imp Q\) and \((\neg Q)\imp (\neg P)\)
  10. \((P\lor Q)\imp R\) and \((P\imp R)\lor (Q\imp R)\)
