Discussion 4.2.1.

We'll let the discussion take us where it may, but here are some suggested talking points to get started.

  • What is matheamtics? What connection do you see between doing mathematics and being human?
  • Think of a time you were captivated by exploring something. What analogies can you draw between doing math and doing this exploration?
  • How does abstraction enrich the meaning of an idea? Give an example from your own experiences.
  • Think of an activity you associate with play. Make a list of all the things you enjoy about its playful aspects. Does your list have analogies in mathematical activities?
  • What role does beauty have in the Christian life?
  • Where is mathematical beauty found in the world?
