Question 5.1.2.

Suppose Casey's Bakery produces some number of pies: 10 contain blueberries, and 15 contain peaches.

  1. What is the largest number of pies Casey's could have produced?
  2. What is the smallest number of pies Casey's could have produced?
  3. What information do we need in order to determine the precise number of pies that Casey's produced?
  4. Present your findings in terms of the cardinalities of certain sets.
